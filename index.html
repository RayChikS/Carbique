Introducing Carbique

Carbique is bold. Carbique is beautiful. And unlike other
products which contribute negatively to the environment,
you only need one of these. In each color.

Fizz at the press of a button

Good for carbonating up to 21 drinks, the patented
Carbonizer magnetically seals to the inside of the lid.
Want to refresh your beverage? Simply set your fizz
level and press the top button.

Keeps your seltzer cold

The base of Carbique contains the world’s smallest
micro-refrigeration technology ever built. Keep your
seltzer at a crisp 36º F for up to 60 hours by
charging with the included base station.

Ultra-lightweight, ultraviolet

Carbique is made from anodized titanium and was
specifically designed to preserve the freshness of
your seltzer. Built-in ultraviolet light kills germs and
keeps your drink clean and pure.
